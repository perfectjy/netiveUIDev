Employee Volunteer Program

Impact the society through volunteering

All employees of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance take part in volunteer work at least once a year to fulfill their responsibilities as members of society.
The company-wide volunteer work that has been carried out since 2005 consists of activities essential to the local community under various themes such as “safety, community, and the environment” for the facilities for children, facilities for the disabled, and facilities for senior citizens nationwide.
In addition, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance's employees voluntarily partake in a variety of volunteer activities, such as those carried out with other family members and talent donation activities using their talents.

Love Sharing Market

Donation of proceeds through bazaars

The Love Sharing Market is a bazaar that has been held by Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance since 2004. To recycle the resources and spread the culture of sharing, articles voluntarily donated by all employees and Hi Planners are collected and sold to the people who need them, and the proceeds are donated to neighbors in need.
It is held across the nation on October 17th, the anniversary of the company foundation, every year.

Financial Literacy Program for Youth

The first step towards the right financial life

This program is jointly held with the Financial Supervisory Service, and the business division located throughout the country makes an alliance with nearby elementary, middle, and high schools to provide financial education to alliance schools.
We provide financial education for students to properly perceive finance, cultivate a sound financial attitude, and have the essential basic common sense when engaging in a financial transaction.

Dream Plus Fund

Employee giving for children with cancer

The Dream Plus Fund is a program designed to reserve part of the salaries of all employees every month to support children and adolescents who have suffered from an illness or injury due to traffic accidents.
Since 1997, we have continued to provide our employees' heartfelt donations and support emotional stability, physical stability, and self-reliance for children and adolescents in need from low-income families to achieve their dream.

Sharing Coffee Zone

Donations for children with leukemia

The Sharing Coffee Zone is a resting space where a donation is made whenever the employees of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance drink a cup of coffee.
1,000 won is donated for every cup straight from a coffee machine installed in the Sharing Coffee Zone, and the donation is relayed to the Korean Association for Children with Leukemia and Cancer (KACLC) to treat children with leukemia and cancer who need our help.
