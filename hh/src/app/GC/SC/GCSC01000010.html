Overview of Social Contribution Activities

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance sincerely hopes that our society will be full of warmer and more beautiful values with all its heart, and it launched an organization dedicated to social contribution activities in 2012 for the first time in the industry, thereby extending its area of social contribution activities.

We place the greatest emphasis on the healthy growth of children and teens, and vigorously take part in the activities designed to provide support for innovation and changes in our society. In addition, we continue to promote mutual growth with local communities and engage in a wide variety of social contribution activities participated by our employees.

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance will contemplate the best ways to address the issues in our society with new ideas and utilize the capacity and assets unique to Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance to the greatest extent to fully engage in addressing social issues.
