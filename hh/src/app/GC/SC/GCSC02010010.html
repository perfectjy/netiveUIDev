Girls Run

Keep the body and mind of girls healthy!

"Girls Run" is a social contribution activity with a new concept of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance to help girls from 3rd to 6th grade grow healthily through running.
Through "Girls Run", female students not only acquire the physical strength and skills to complete long-distance runs, but also form harmonious relationships with others with the right attitude and develop a good personality to contribute towards our society.

A Very Little Confession

Speak out against school violence

To combat school violence at its source, which has emerged as one of the biggest issues in Korean society in recent years, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has signed an agreement with the Ministry of Education and a youth violence prevention foundation to provide "A Very Little Confession"
It is a social contribution project that helps teens to heal a variety of concerns and wounds caused by school violence, career issues, and relationships with friends and parents through the comfort obtained from the people who listen to them.

Heart Rest

A program for children with disabilities and their families

"Heart Rest" is a program in which caregivers look after children with disabilities for certain hours instead of their guardians.
It consists of "one-to-one care support" in which a caregiver visits the hospital in person to look after children with disabilities and manages their treatment schedules, and "play classroom", which uses music and art tools to help them develop language skills and physical senses.
This program offers children with disabilities new pleasure and their guardians time to relax or deal with any chores they have put off.

Enactus

Entrepreneurship for a better world

Enactus(Entrepreneurial. Action. Us.) is a community of students, professors, and entrepreneurs who change lives and create a sustainable and better world through the practice of entrepreneurship. College students at Enactus work with supervisors and entrepreneurs to implement projects designed to improve the quality of life and standard of living of those in need in local communities.
Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance sponsors college students to help them grow into practical business leaders with a sense of social responsibility while creating a better world.

Devote Like a Journalist

Write to change the world

While public interest in the non-profit field has recently grown at a tremendous pace in Korea, there are insufficient journalists with deep insight in this field, and therefore, various social issues are often not examined in the right direction and fail to garner public attention.
Therefore, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance develops an educational program with a new concept that nurtures "social editors", the experts who process and deliver the news associated with the non-profit sector and corporate social contribution activities with the Chosun Ilbo for a better future.

Hi Challenge School

Right growth of youth through challenges and practices

This project is designed to help youth pay attention to their surroundings as members of a local community and develop the power of the mind to create a better world.
Under the theme of "Empathizing with myself, others, and the world", this project is composed of the <bringing up the mind> class that seeks to discover issues and countermeasures with a focus on how to exert a good influence over a wide range of people around and the <sharing minds> class that implement the measures.
These two classes allow youth to discover their potential and gain confidence in the process of paying attention to and addressing the surrounding problems while learning the value of relationships in the process of communicating with friends. Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance helps the right growth of youth and supports them to grow into adults who contribute to society in the future.

Heart Library

Provide rest areas to local hospitals

Since 2015, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has engaged in social contribution activities through a program called "Heart Library" by providing high-quality books and cultural spaces to local hospitals.
Patients and their guardians can find emotional stability and recharge their emotional batteries to cope with hospital setting, illness or medical procedures. In addition, the hospital in which the library is built provides cultural and artistic programs for patients and their guardians, thereby offering peace of mind and joy.

Gwanghwamun Concert

Free performance once every month

The "Gwanghwamun Concert" is an admission-free music performance held in an idle space in the Gwanghwamun area. This is a social contribution activity of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance designed to provide a venue for performances to artists and to introduce the various aspects of culture and art to the audience.
It unveils a wide variety of music genres such as classical music, jazz, popular music, and Korean traditional music once every month.

Salvation Army

Donation to help people in need every year

To ensure the warm winter of the marginalized neighbors in need and to take part in the beautiful touch of the Salvation Army, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance provides a donation to the Salvation Army every year.
In addition, the company takes an active role in fund-raising by installing the Salvation Army's red kettle in front of its headquarters in Gwanghwamun, Seoul.
As a result of this continuous and active donation, the company joined the Salvation Army’s "Best Donor Club" and received an appreciation plaque for the "Best Shield Donor".
The "Best Donor Club" is a new system in effect by the Salvation Army to provide special treatment for major donors.
