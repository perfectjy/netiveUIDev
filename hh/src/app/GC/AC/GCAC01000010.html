A Company that Values Society and the Environment

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance was incorporated in March 1955 as a comprehensive marine insurance company under the corporate mission of "national economic development" and "promotion of public welfare."

Since then, as a leader in the non-life insurance industry, the company has continued to provide a social safety net throughout all categories of non-life insurance, including marine, fire, auto, casualty, long-term, annuity, and retirement insurance (pension).

Even at this very moment, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance remains fully dedicated to fulfilling its social responsibilities to its customers, shareholders, employees, local communities, and the nation by putting customer satisfaction first in the center of all of its business activities, such as product development, underwriting service, and the compensation service.
