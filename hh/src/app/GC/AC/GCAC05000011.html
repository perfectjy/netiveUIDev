"Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance will continue to support the fulfillment of your dreams and happiness."

This is Yong-Il Cho, CEO of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance.

Being aware that thee trust of the customers was our most value asset, we became the first in the industry to declare the Charter for Customer Satisfaction, and under the brand slogan, "Hearts that Care", we are constantly endeavoring to provide the best service.

We will remain fully committed to helping our customers realize their dreams and happiness by listening attentively to their wishes and reflecting them in our products and services.

Thank you.
