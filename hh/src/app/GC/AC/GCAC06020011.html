HiLife

Highlife of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance is a long-term insurance brand that embodies the "service spirit that always protects customers right by their side to ensure that they can enjoy a leisurely and safe life". The three circles that emanate free touch in the English logotype designed to communicate stability and trust which signify our customers’ precious happiness (orange), affluent life (green), and bright future (blue).
