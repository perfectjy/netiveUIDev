Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance heart font

It is a font for headlines only developed to express a warm and attentive corporate image that brings a smile to the customers’ faces.

An example image of the Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance heart font.

  • Rolled the horizontal strokes of consonants into a circle to shape the element of a "smile".
  • The upper part forms a straight visual baseline, while the lower part is given a sense of rhythm by changing the font height to emanate a sense of vitality.
  • Created characters that resemble a garden to communicate a bright and cheerful feeling.
  • Expressed the warm and attentive sensibility unique to Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance for the customers

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance gothic font

As a font that uses clear and straight lines to improve readability, and it is used exclusively for the main text, and its vertical stroke which extends straight down embodies the honest and strong entrepreneurial spirit.

  • Realized the clearest and most natural form that put a stable composition on the paper and readability as the top priority
  • In line with the shape principles of Hangeul, the length is longer than the width.
  • Moved the position of the consonants and created a layout that breaks away from the rectangular frame Moved the position of the consonants and created a layout that breaks away from the rectangular frame to communicate a bright and modern feeling
  • Reinforced the word recognition rate by reducing the space between characters.
  • Expressed the strong and honest corporate culture of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance
