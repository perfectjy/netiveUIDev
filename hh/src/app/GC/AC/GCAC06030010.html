The corporate brand slogan “With all our hearts” was created to facilitate the understanding of the corporate brand image of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance which strives to satisfy customers with integrity. The brand slogan embodies the corporate spirit of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance, which remains sincere to its customers with all its heart as an insurance expert. It is not designed to simply describe the means and methods of a company that wishes to provide services properly but to provide a hearty expression of the sincerity of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance.
