Environmental(E)

Based on the philosophy of the eco-friendly management of "a company that loves the environment and respects its values", Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance continues to promote various eco-friendly activities to fulfill its environmental responsibilities.

Company-wide Energy-saving Activities

As energy conservation has emerged as an important agenda for environmental issues, the company carries out various activities to prevent unnecessary energy consumption and practice energy conservation. To minimize the environmental burden created from corporate activities and to take part in the national energy conservation campaign, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance actively carries out the activities to reduce electricity consumption in the summer and winter and distribute guidelines on the practice of energy conservation each year within the company.

Development and Sales of Eco-friendly Insurance Products

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance develops a wide range of products and services to contribute towards carbon emissions reduction. The representative eco-friendly insurance products that Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance offers include the Auto Eco Mileage Special Contract, Daily Special Contract, EcoPoint Reward Special Contract, Hicar Green Service Special Contract, Hilife Power Eco Driver Insurance, and Green Love Bike Casualty Insurance. With these eco-friendly insurance products, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance strives to realize the practice of active environmental management required of companies and to satisfy customer needs for eco-friendly services.
Hicar Eco Auto Insurance, the first product using used parts launched in the insurance industry, has been recognized for its originality and obtained an exclusive license. In addition, the Hicar Green Service Special Contract replaced insurance contract data such as terms and conditions, and securities, which had been provided as printed documents, with e-mail, and the reduced business expenses are returned to the policyholders through premium discount benefits. To support the government's policy to foster the purchase of electric vehicles, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has developed insurance policies for electric vehicles for sale for the first time in the insurance industry.

Response to Climate Change

In July 2010, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance established the Research Center for Transportation, Climate, and Environment for the first time in the insurance industry, and since then, the company has researched the climate and environment for the sustainable growth of the insurance industry by fully responding to climate change and environmental issues.
As the risk of natural disasters has recently increased due to climate change, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance is protecting the lives and properties of our customers from extreme climate events such as typhoons, torrential rains, and heavy snowfalls. As a leading example, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has installed water level meters and notification equipment in areas prone to floods to inform the customers of the risk in real-time in the event of a flood caused by torrential rain to prevent accidents or minimize resultant damage.

Environmental Performance Disclosure

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance discloses environmental performance information, such as water and energy consumption, waste, greenhouse gas, and air and water pollutant generated at its business establishments.

Governance(G)

Governance Transparency

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance strives to create a transparent business environment by continuously improving corporate governance and makes the utmost effort to protect and promote the rights and interests of all stakeholders by establishing and operating an independent board of directors and monitoring bodies. As the highest decision-making body in the company, the BOD of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance is composed in accordance with the relevant laws and regulations, such as the Commercial Act and the Act on Corporate Governance of Financial Companies.
In addition, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance operates four subcommittees (Audit Committee, Executive Candidate Nomination Committee, Risk Management Committee, and Remuneration Committee) to ensure the expertise and independence of the BOD.

Learn more about corporate governance

Ethical Management

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has enacted the ethics charter and the code of ethics and formulated rules on the work execution for its employees and board members as the code of conduct to practice ethical management that fulfills its responsibilities and obligations for its customers, employees, and board members, shareholders, partners, and local communities. To ensure the concrete and substantive realization of ethical management, the company has the practice guidelines for the Code of Ethics in effect, and forges ahead with ethical compliance management by appointing a compliance officer who oversees compliance and the operation of the internal control system through the deliberation of the BOD.

Fair Operation and Trade

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance pushes ahead with fair trade compliance activities such as enacting and operating the Regulations on Voluntary Compliance of Fair Trade to achieve business performance through fair and free competition. In addition, the company conducts periodic inspections and provides training for departments with related work to eradicate wrongful unfair conducts such as rigging prices or trade conditions and prevents unfair trade such as unfairly taking advantage of the position in the trade so as to comply with the Monopoly Regulation and Fair Trade Act.
Since 2018, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has also drafted the "Agreement for the Establishment of a Mutual Prosperity Culture" when signing a contract to create a fair and reasonable trade culture with business partners and achieve development based on mutual prosperity. As a result, the company continues to eradicate unfair trade that abuse superior positions, improper solicitation or instructions, and requests for money and valuables. Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance is set to create an environment for mutual growth premised on trust with business partners as well as a clean corporate culture.

Risk Management and Response

Under the rapidly changing global financial environment, as uncertainty in the global economy and the complexity of the financial system continues to rise, the importance of risk management activities is further brought into focus. Therefore, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance will continue to upgrade its risk management system and seek the balance between risk and profit to expand the company-wide business culture of risk management based on the advancement of the risk management organization, regulations, and systems.
Risk management is carried out in the order of company-wide risk planning, risk measurement, and analysis, risk management execution, reporting, utilization, and evaluation. The company formulates mid- to long-term plans for company-wide risk management and quantifies and analyzes the risks acknowledged in the overall business activities such as insurance sales and asset management. In addition, to control and reduce quantified risks, the company draws management plans through close cooperation with the relevant departments, follows procedures for execution through internal reports, inspects the execution history of risk management plans, and continues to provide feedback on the results.
