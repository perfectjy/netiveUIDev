ESG Strategy System

ESG stands for environmental, social, and governance, and it refers to non-financial factors that affect business.

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has formulated the ESG implementation mission and tasks to set the ESG management as the core corporate task and ensure that all employees implement it in the field.

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has set "a company that contributes towards the sustainable development of humankind" as its ESG management mission. This is an expression of our adamant will to clearly recognize that the ultimate goal of ESG management is the sustainable development of humankind and to actively pursue this goal.

As the three areas of ESG management and the major tasks to be implemented, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance presents "eco-friendly management that preserves the environment" in the environmental (E) area, "social value management for the social community" in the social (S) area, and "transparent and ethical business management" in the governance (G) area.

ESG governance

The board of directors and management of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance closely cooperate and communicate with the working organization for ESG and lead the way for the group's activities for the ESG improvement.

ESG Management Committee

To respond to changes in the ESG management paradigm early on, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has established the ESG Management Committee. This committee is chaired by the CEO and is composed executive officers who serve as the head of each group and division pertinent to ESG management activities. The ESG Management Committee is designed to establish company-wide ESG-related strategies and policies, monitor and manage the performance of tasks, and present and report on important matters to the BOD.

ESG Steering Committee

The ESG Steering Committee of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance is a consultative body designed to establish the goals and tasks for company-wide ESG-related policy formulation and risk management, direction establishment for the mid- to long-term response to climate change, and the expansion of eco-friendly investment.
The committee is chaired by the head of the Digital Strategy Division and is composed of the heads of the relevant departments. The ESG Steering Committee pursues the growth of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance as a healthy and sustainable company as a member of society and continues to reinforce its executive ability through the advice given by affiliated working-level councils and external experts.

ESG Strategy TFT (an organization dedicated to ESG)

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance established the ESG strategy TFT in charge of ESG management under the Digital Strategy Division (December, 2021) The ESG Strategy TFT provides support for the EST Steering Committee and manages the tasks designed to implement ESG and associated performance.
