About the institute

Always Sunny!

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance has led the way for the non-life insurance industry since its inception!
It has established the Research Center for Transportation, Climate, and Environment to make history. The future weather map of Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance, which vigorously braces itself for and responds to the rapidly changing environment, is "always sunny."

The Research Center for Transportation, Climate, and Environment is designed to recognize climate change as a threat as well as an opportunity, to formulate preemptive countermeasures, to practice corporate social responsibility through research on traffic safety, which constitutes a traditional non-life insurance field, and to lead the way for the development of the insurance industry with mid- to long-term research on the insurance environment.

Through specialized research on the major areas of the insurance industry, such as climate and environment, transportation, and finance, as well as research converging the different research sectors, it serves an active role for Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance to create a unique and differentiated performance, and it will continue to grow as a specialized research center that will drive the company's mid- to long-term growth.

Traffic safety field

In response to changes in the traffic environment through creative research, research center will ensure customer safety and increase the value.

Research center conducts research to ensure customer safety and prevent social loss based on changes in the traffic environment across the three key areas of traffic safety: human, vehicle, and road environment.

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance aims to suggest visions for future transportation with customer service and innovative research that will pave the way for the industry on the plan to prevent and reduce traffic accidents for protecting the safe and happy lives of our customers, the improvement in the traffic policy and system to combat traffic issues, the plan to deal with changes in the traffic environment such as the emergence of future transportation means.

Climate and environment field

Through more extensive research on climate change,
research center will lead the way for early response and risk management.

Climate change brought by global warming causes typhoons, heavy snowfalls, droughts, and floods throughout the world, and personnel and property damage has also recently increased in Korea due to extreme climate events and natural disasters, such as an earthquake that occurred around Pohang and Gyeongju.

Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance is the first in the insurance industry to research the climate and environment to recognize climate change as both a crisis and an opportunity and to respond early on.

  • Research on legal and institutional changes

Climate change/natural disaster risk management

  • Establishment of the risk management infrastructure
  • Research on associated laws and systems
  • Operation of the weather information service

Research on new risks

  • Forecast and analysis of new risks
  • Research on risk management measures

Product market research

  • New product and new market research
  • Overseas market research

Finance and insurance field

Through the analysis and research of rapidly changing financial environment, research center will provide mid- to long-term strategies to further develop the insurance industry.

Market changes surrounding the financial industry, including the insurance industry, continue to gain speed, and as a result, uncertainty is also growing. Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance will effectively respond to such changes and uncover new growth engines based on profitability to deal with the increasingly fierce global competition.

Research center plays a role as a think tank that paves the way for finance and insurance through converged research based on information and communications technology, including the long-term insurance field that serves as the major growth engine.
